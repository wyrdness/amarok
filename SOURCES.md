## Respect & Cultural Sensitivity

### Consulting with Communities

When researching Inuit traditional knowledge:
- Consult with Inuit communities and cultural authorities
- Respect intellectual property of oral traditions
- Recognize traditional knowledge as living practice
- Follow OCAP principles (Ownership, Control, Access, Possession)

### Recommended Guidelines

- Inuit Tapiriit Kanatami. *National Inuit Strategy on Research*. (2018)
- ITK & NRI. *Negotiating Research Relationships with Inuit Communities: A Guide for Researchers*. (2007)

## Cultural Sensitivity Statement

This documentation is based on publicly available sources about Inuit traditional knowledge. We recognize that Inuit oral traditions are the intellectual and cultural property of Inuit peoples. Researchers and readers should consult with Inuit communities and respect their guidance on the use and representation of traditional knowledge.
